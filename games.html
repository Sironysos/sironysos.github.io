My Favorite Games

This is a list of the games I enjoy playing daily.

Binary puzzle

My favorite game: you have to place 0s and 1s in a grid according to specific rules. You can play the daily puzzle, and the special puzzles are also very fun!

Worldle

You probably know the Wordle game from NYT. This is NOT Wordle, it's a geography-based game in which you have to guess the location of a country based on its outline.

Waffle

This game is a word puzzle game where you have to fit words into a grid, by swaping letters. You could learn new words by looking at the corresponding definition when you complete the challenge.

Nerdle

This game is a math-based puzzle game where you have to guess a hidden equation. It's a fun way to challenge your math skills!

Metazooa

In this game, you have to find the daily animal based on its zoological family.

Genitle

Find the genital organ based on its description. It's a fun way to learn more about human anatomy witout any taboo.

Flagle

In this game, you have to guess the flag of a country.

Angle

Find the angle in blue. You only have 4 guesses.

Anti wordle

This is kind of the contrary of wordle. In this game, you have to last as long as possible without guessing the word.

Wiki Trivia

Test your knowledge with cards that you have to place chronologically.

Bandle

Find the song with as little instruments as possible.

When taken

In this game, you have to guess the date and the place a photo was taken.

Language Guesser

You have a music clip and you have to find the language used in.

Travle

Connect two countries with as little contries in between as possible.

Geo Connections

There are 16 tiles cards, and you have to group them in groups of 4.

Geo Grid

Find rarest countries with two features asked in the column and the row. VBe carful, a country can only be picked one time.

Elementle

Find the right chemical element.

Tradle

Find the country based on its major exports.

Couplit

To win, arrange all letters so they spell out a complete phrase based on the day's theme. You can swap letters vertically and horizontally.

Hexcodle

In this game, you have to guess the hex color code based on the given clues.

Nanagrams

You have to guess the maximum operations possible using the compulsory operators.

Tetris Digit Drop

Just like Tetris, except that the blocks are made up of digits, and rows can be cleared by forming valid equations.

Snake Adder

Like snake, you have to guide a snake to collect numbers and reach a target sum.

Semantle

In this game, you have to guess a word, and there are hints in a map, based on semantic relationships.

Pedantix

Guess the Wikipedia article title by its summary.

Balatrodle

Guess the Balatro joker.
